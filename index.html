Occulture is a blending of the words "occult" and "culture" popularized by Christopher Partridge - a fusion which signifies the ethereal influence they exert between each other.

The occult deeply influences our culture, but how does culture influence the occult? What of the sub-cultures moving under the surface, the citizens of the esoteric circles of our world?

Join your will with ours this November in Berlin.
