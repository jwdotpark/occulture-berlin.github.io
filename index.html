During the conference, we will have several spaces in which artists can exhibit their work on themes related to esoterica. If you'd like to exhibit your work, please send us your portfolio as well as a description of the work you intend to exhibit - you should include details such as the medium, dimensions, selling price (if you wish to sell your work), and a brief artist's statement.

One evening will be dedicated to divination of all types. During this evening participants will be able to try out different kinds of divination, such as tarot, astrology, palmistry, stone gazing and many more.

If you'd like to offer divinatory services during this evening, please contact us!

Occulture is a blending of the words "occult" and "culture" popularized by Christopher Partridge - a fusion which signifies the ethereal influence they exert between each other.

The occult deeply influences our culture, but how does culture influence the occult? What of the sub-cultures moving under the surface, the citizens of the esoteric circles of our world?

Join your will with ours this November in Berlin.
