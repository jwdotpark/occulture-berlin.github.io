Tigre Contre Tigre
The music and stage performance of Tigre Contre Tigre tends to open a specific mental and temporal space. Through musical rituals made of machines and voices, they trigger symbiosis and leap into the unknown. Primal, futuristic, mystic and electronic; so can be described these soft and brutal performances.
