Caught Between Worlds
Beny Tchaicovsky
Immerse yourself in unfathomable worlds of Marvel and mystery through the cutting-edge of 3D computer animation art. CAUGHT BETWEEN WORLDS is a journey into worlds which can only be described through technology. Sit back and give yourself up as the world of every day is inexplicably blended into the world of imagination. Caught Between Worlds is the second part of the Morning Star Trilogy, preceded by "The Call" and followed by "Dimensional Connections".
