Re-Remembering
Virtual Awakening x Imaginarium Theater x ritual Maya
A multi-sensorial dance between the worlds of ancient and modern technologies. Open your senses, explore with playfulness all the stimulus we will provide you, let your spirit to remember how wise and strong it is. Join us into this mystical journey through XIBALBA (Mayan underworld) and other multiverses full of eco-awareness. And allow the old version of your self to die in order to reborn in this new era of RITUALITY. The story revolves around ceremony, sacred animals, warriors and underworld. The messages are delivered in the indigenous Mayan languages of Tzotzil and Lacandon. Our music is played with ancestral instruments and our costumes are authentic.
